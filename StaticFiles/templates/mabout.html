About CollegePlus™

CollegePlus

CollegePlus is a web-application made by class of 2019 high school graduates. The creators made this website because they had a terrible experience finding a college. We all have friends and people at our high schools who graduated not knowing what they should study and they had trouble finding a good college to attend. Many of them just ended up settling. It is never good to settle for something so important. College is one of the most defining experiences in life and the college we attend should match our personality. CollegePlus aims to solve all the issues with the college search process. We want to be the swiss army knife that simplifies the college process and puts all college resources in one place.
